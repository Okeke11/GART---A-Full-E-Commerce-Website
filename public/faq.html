Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on Gart.

How do I start selling on Gart?

To start selling, create an account and click on the "Sell" button in the menu. You'll need to upload details of your product, set a price, and add clear images. Once approved, your item will be live on the marketplace.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location and seller. Standard delivery within major cities typically takes 3-5 business days, while express options are available for 1-2 day delivery.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers, and all payments are processed through secure gateways.

Can I return an item?

Yes, we offer a 7-day return policy for items that are defective or not as described. Please visit our "Returns" page for the full terms and conditions.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via our Contact Us page, email us at support@gart.io, or call our helpline. Our team is available Mon-Fri from 8 AM to 6 PM.
